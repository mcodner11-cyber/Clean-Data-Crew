PURPOSE OF THE PROGRAM

A menu-driven Point of Sale (POS) System for Best Buy Retail Store. It allows
cashiers to manage a shopping cart, apply discounts and tax, accept payment,
and generate a receipt. Multiple transactions can be processed in one session.

HOW TO RUN THE PROGRAM


Steps:
  1. Open a terminal and navigate to the folder containing the file.
  2. Run: CLEAN DATA CREW-POS-ITT103-SP2026.py 
       1 - View Products
       2 - Add Item to Cart
       3 - Remove Item from Cart
       4 - View Cart
       5 - Checkout
       6 - Exit


MODIFICATION 1 - Product Catalog
  Added a list of 12 products (dictionaries) with name, price, and stock.

MODIFICATION 2 - View Products
  Displays all products with prices and stock. Shows low-stock warning
  for items below 5 units.

MODIFICATION 3 - Add Item to Cart
  Searches by name, validates stock, and updates quantity if already in cart.

MODIFICATION 4 - Remove Item from Cart
  Removes items fully or partially and restores stock accordingly.

MODIFICATION 5 - View Cart
  Displays cart items with quantity, unit price, line total, and cart total.

MODIFICATION 6 - Checkout & Payment
  Calculates subtotal, applies 5% discount (over $5,000), adds 10% tax,
  validates payment, and computes change.

MODIFICATION 7 - Receipt Generation
  Prints a formatted receipt with all transaction details and a thank-you note.

MODIFICATION 8 - Low-Stock Alerts
  Alerts shown in the product list and after adding an item drops stock below 5.

MODIFICATION 9 - Input Validation
  Validates quantity and payment inputs; re-prompts on invalid entries.

Assumptions:
  - Prices are in Jamaican dollars (JMD).
  - Discount is applied before tax.
  - Product names must be spelled correctly (case-insensitive).

Limitations:
  - No data is saved when the program closes.
  - No login system or transaction history.
  - Product catalog cannot be edited during a session.
